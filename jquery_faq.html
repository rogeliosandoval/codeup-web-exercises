Redwood State National Park

  • Redwood National Park covers a total area of 131,983 acres
  • Redwood was made a national park on October 2, 1968
  • The lowest elevation found in Redwood is 0 feet at the Pacific Ocean
  • Redwood National Park lies in the Pacific time zone

Spring Mountains

  • There are 27 named mountains in the Spring Mountains
  • Most of the high peaks in the Spring Mountains are found in the northern portion of the range
  • Home to over 35 species of tree, the Spring Mountains are said to be the most biologically diverse of all Nevada's mountain ranges
  • The sandstone bluffs of Red Rock Canyon National Conservation Area dominate the middle portion of the Spring Mountains

Hot Springs National Park

  • Hot Springs National Park covers a total area of 5,550 acres
  • The lowest elevation found in Hot Springs is 415 feet at Bull Bayou
  • Hot Springs National Park lies in the Central time zone
  • The number of people visiting Hot Springs in 2019 was 1,467,153
